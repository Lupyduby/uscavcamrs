AV Center Guidelines

Your ooperation is needed in maintaining an orderly and clean hall.


Please be reminded of the following:

  • Eating, drinking and smoking are not allowed inside the hall.
  • Put cellular phone in silent mode.
  • Raising legs on the backrest of the person sitting in front of you shall not be tolerated.
  • Sharing of seat with a friend or classmate is not allowed.
  • Routine checking before and after each activity will be done; any damage discovered will be charged to the users/organizers of the activity.
  • Nails, thumbtacks and pins, writing pens, double-sided tape or any material that can damage the stage, walls, chairs, and backdrop is not allowed.
  • Clean-up of the back stage is expected after every activity by the organizer. Props left inside the hall or backstage will be thrown away right after the activity.
  • Bringing in personal equipment such as laptop, LCD, lapel microphones, lights, etc. Should be referred to AV Librarian/AV technician for appropriate use guidelines and charges.
  • Acts of vandalism or any damage done intentionally or maliciously on any part of the hall shall be dealt with accordingly.

"A Clean and well functioning AV Hall benefits everyone"

Thank you very much!
